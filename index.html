About

Who We Are

The Crown Vics are Maine's rock 'n' roll dance band. Built on the bones of rockabilly, boogie woogie, and honky tonk — twisted through classic rock, Americana, indie, and whatever else catches their ear. They've played everywhere from Portland to Halifax, from ski resorts to theaters, from corporate events to bar stages. No long solos. No slow fades. Hardly a breath between numbers. Every night is a show. Every show is an experience.
